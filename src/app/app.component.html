About Me


My name is Ryan Hernandez and I am a senior in the College of Engineering at the University of Oklahoma, graduating with a degree in Computer Science in May 2020. I grew up in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, but also lived for three years in Spokane, Washington, where I fell in love with the Pacific Northwest. Because of my love for the area, I've made the decision to seek employment from one of the many amazing companies based in Seattle.


I grew up during the dot com bubble that saw the rapid adoption of technology and the explosion of personal computing, and as a result, my interest in computers and passion for learning new technologies was cultivated at an early age. To describe myself, I am a gregarious person that enjoys making new friends and working as a team to solve challenging problems.
Beyond my passion for technology, I am also incredibly passionate about music (of a variety of genres), and sports such as basketball, soccer, and football.


I am always eager to learn new technologies, and have been fortunate enough to have the opportunity to do just that, as well as improve my problem solving skills and coding abilities, while working at my internship at American Fidelity.
I've mainly worked in full stack development of web applications in my professional life, however through my education I also have experience from classes in artificial intelligence and machine learning, as well as in computer security. Additionally, next semester I will be taking a course in intelligent robotics.
In my personal life, I have worked on some projects (that I will expand upon further below) involving data analytics and iOS development.
